How to Make Ice cream

Description:

This ice cream recipe is incredibly basic and straightforward. Following this recipe will result in exceptionally tasty ice cream! This recipe calls for everyday ingredients that you may get at your local grocery store or at home! If you don't follow the recipe exactly, you'll end up with an unpleasant, lethal concoction for your tastebuds. So, what are you holding out for? Go further into this website to discover how to create ice cream quickly!

Materials/Ingredients:

  • Measuring Cup
  • Measuring Spoon
  • Big Ziplock
  • Ziplock(Smaller than big ziplock)
  • Halite(Pink Salt)
  • Ice
  • Whipping Cream(35% milk)
  • Milk(2-3%)
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Large bowl
  • Hand Whisk

Steps:

  1. Get your measuring cup, and pour 1 cup of milk in the cup. Then carefully pour the milk from the cup into the bowl.
  2. Get your measuring cup. Then pour 1 cup of whipping cream into it. Then take your cup and carefully pour the cream from the cup.
  3. Get your measuring cup and then pour ¼ cup sugar In the cup. Then pour the sugar from the cup into the bowl.
  4. Get your Measuring Spoon, and pour 1 tsp(Tea spoon) of vanilla extract into the spoon. Then pour the vanilla extract from the spoon into the bo
  5. Rinse your measuring cup thoroughly.
  6. Grab your big ziplock bag and open it. Then get your measuring it cup and put 1 cup of ice in the cup.
  7. Pour the ice from the measuring cup into the large ziplock bag. Then repeat this process 3 more times.
  8. Get your measuring cup and pour ⅔ Pink Salt. Then pour the pink salt from the cup into the bag.
  9. Close the ziplock and begin to shake it for 5-10 seconds.
  10. Take your mixture from the bowl and pour it into the small ziplock.
  11. Close the small ziplock and insert it into the large one.
  12. Close the large ziplock very tight, and wrap it around a towel.
  13. Shake the bag really hard in a fast rapid motion For 10 minutes.
  14. After 10 minutes open the ziplock and take out the small ziplock. Then wash the small ziplock in cold water for 5-6 seconds.
  15. Open the small ziplock, and enjoy!
